Glossary

One Grand Account (OGA)
Federal savings account with a $1,000 seed for eligible births. Contributions start July 4, 2026. Growth is tax‑deferred.
Qualified use
Education/job training, first-time home purchase, or major medical/disability expenses.
Contribution cap
Maximum yearly contribution. For OGAs: $5,000/year; employer up to $2,500 counts toward the cap (pending rules).
Index fund
Low-cost fund that tracks a market index.
Tax-deferred
Taxes apply later, typically on withdrawal.
