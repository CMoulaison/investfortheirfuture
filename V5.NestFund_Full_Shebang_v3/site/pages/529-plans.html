529 Plans

State-sponsored education accounts. Washington offers DreamAhead and GET.

DreamAhead

  • Investment-based. Market returns apply.
  • Usable nationwide for qualified costs.

GET (Guaranteed Education Tuition)

  • Prepaid tuition. Tied to WA tuition rates.
  • Helps protect against tuition inflation.
