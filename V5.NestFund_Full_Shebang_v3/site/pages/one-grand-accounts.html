One Grand Account

OGAs open July 4, 2026 for eligible births (2025–2028). $1,000 seed. Up to $5,000/year contributions. Growth is tax‑deferred.

Eligibility

  • Born on or after Jan 1, 2025 (exact windows subject to final guidance).

How it works

  • Federal seed: $1,000 at setup.
  • Private contributions: up to $5,000/year (after-tax).
  • Employer contributions: up to $2,500/year (counts toward total cap) — pending Treasury/IRS rules.
  • Investments: low-cost index funds/ETFs.

Uses after 18

  • Education / job training
  • First-time home purchase
  • Major medical or disability expenses
Note: Weddings and general life events are not qualified uses. “Business startup” appears in some summaries but is not confirmed in regulations.

Age 30/31 rules

Non‑qualified withdrawals before 30 may face a 10% additional tax plus income tax on earnings. At 31, the account may be treated as distributed if unused (awaiting final guidance).
