NestFund Starter Guide

This guide helps you choose a weekly deposit and set up a simple plan. You’ll also get our 13‑week course (two emails/week).

1) Understand OGAs

$1,000 seed. Up to $5,000/year in contributions. Growth is tax‑deferred. Accounts open July 4, 2026.

2) Try the calculator

Visit the OGA page and test $10, $25, $50 per week with/without the seed and cap.

3) Automate deposits

Weekly or twice‑weekly transfers from checking. Small and steady wins.

4) When your child turns 18

Use funds for education/job training, a first home, or major medical expenses. Non‑qualified uses may face taxes/penalties.

5) Age 30/31 rules

Before 30: non‑qualified withdrawals may face a 10% additional tax plus income tax. At 31: balance may be treated as distributed (awaiting guidance).
