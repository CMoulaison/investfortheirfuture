Glossary

One Grand Account (OGA)
Federal savings account for eligible births (2025–2028) with a $1,000 seed; contributions start July 4, 2026. Growth is tax-deferred.
Tax-deferred
Taxes are paid later, usually when money is withdrawn, not while it grows.
Qualified use
Education/job training, first-time home purchase, or major medical/disability expenses.
Index fund
A fund that tracks a market index to keep costs low and returns aligned to the market.
Contribution cap
Maximum you can contribute in a year. For OGAs: $5,000/year; employer up to $2,500 counts toward the cap (awaiting final guidance).
