What’s a One Grand Account?

Starting in 2025, qualifying newborns receive a $1,000 federally seeded investment account tied to the S&P 500. These are called One Grand Accounts.

  • Born on or after Jan 1, 2025
  • Income under 200% of federal poverty level
  • Tax-deferred growth
  • Accessible at age 18 — can be transferred into a Traditional IRA, Roth IRA, or education account

What If You Don’t Qualify?

There are still great options like 529 Plans, UTMA accounts, and Roth IRAs for minors. We’ll help you choose what fits.
