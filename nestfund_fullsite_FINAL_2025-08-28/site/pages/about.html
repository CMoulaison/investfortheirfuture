Why I built NestFund

Becoming a grandparent made me see my gaps in financial literacy. I’ve worked hard for decades, but I didn’t have a simple system for saving and investing. I want parents and grandparents like me to have a place to learn clear steps, see the math, and build habits that compound.

This site is our shared project. We teach, we calculate, and we practice small weekly deposits. I’m learning in public so more families can set kids up for the future. Let’s learn and grow together.
