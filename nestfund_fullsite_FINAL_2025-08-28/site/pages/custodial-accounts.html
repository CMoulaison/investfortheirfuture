Custodial accounts (UTMA/UGMA)

Accounts owned by a minor and controlled by an adult until the age of majority. Flexible uses but may impact financial aid.

Pros

  • Broad investment choices
  • Simple setup

Cautions

  • Counts as the child’s asset
  • Possible kiddie tax
