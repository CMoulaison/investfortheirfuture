Glossary

Tax‑deferred
Taxes are paid when you withdraw, not while the money grows.
Qualified distribution
An allowed withdrawal purpose that avoids penalties.
Annual contribution limit
The most you can add in a year from all sources combined.
Custodial account
An account owned by a minor but managed by an adult.
