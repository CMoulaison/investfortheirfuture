What is the One Grand Account?

The OGA is a federal program expected to launch July 4, 2026. Eligible children (born on or after Jan 1, 2025) receive a $1,000 seed at birth. Families can contribute up to $5,000 per year from all sources combined. Employers may contribute within that annual cap. Growth is tax‑deferred.

Allowable uses

  • Education and skills training
  • First‑home purchase
  • Rollover to retirement at age 18+

Non‑qualified uses may face taxes and penalties. Some details await final federal guidance.

Key facts

  • Eligibility: child born on/after Jan 1, 2025
  • Seed: $1,000 at setup
  • Annual cap: $5,000 (all sources)
  • Growth: tax‑deferred
  • Availability: expected July 4, 2026
