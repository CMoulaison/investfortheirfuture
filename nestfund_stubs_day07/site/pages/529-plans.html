529 Plans

529 plans are state-sponsored accounts for education expenses. Washington offers DreamAhead and GET.

DreamAhead GET Pros Cautions Quick links

DreamAhead

  • Investment-based. Value depends on markets.
  • Flexible. Usable nationwide for qualified costs.

GET (Guaranteed Education Tuition)

  • Prepaid tuition. Tied to Washington tuition rates.
  • Helps protect against tuition inflation.

Pros

  • Earnings are tax-free if used for qualified education expenses.
  • High contribution limits.

Cautions

  • Non-qualified withdrawals may face taxes and penalties.
  • Investment returns are not guaranteed in DreamAhead.
