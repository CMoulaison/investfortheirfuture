529 Plans

Save for education the tax‑advantaged way

529 plans are state‑sponsored accounts for qualified education costs. Washington offers DreamAhead and GET.

DreamAhead

  • Investment‑based; market returns vary.
  • Usable nationwide for qualified expenses.

GET (Guaranteed Education Tuition)

  • Prepaid tuition tied to Washington tuition rates.
  • Helps protect against tuition inflation.
