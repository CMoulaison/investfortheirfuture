529 Plans

Save for education the tax advantaged way

529 plans are state sponsored accounts for education. Washington offers DreamAhead and GET.

DreamAhead

  • Investment based; market returns vary.
  • Use nationwide for qualified education costs.

GET (Guaranteed Education Tuition)

  • Prepaid tuition tied to WA rates.
  • Helps protect against tuition inflation.
