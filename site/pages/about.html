Our story

Learning together, building for the next generation

I became a dad at seventeen and went straight to work. I did not learn how to build wealth, and I did not teach it. When my granddaughter was born and One Grand Accounts launched, I realized I needed to change that — for my family and for others like us.

NestFund is my way of learning out loud. I am studying One Grand Accounts, 529s, and custodial accounts and sharing what I learn in plain language so real families can take action. This is not Wall Street. It is a working dad and grandpa figuring it out with you.

If you have ever thought, “I wish someone taught me this sooner,” you are in the right place. Let us learn and grow together.
