Custodial Accounts

Flexible investing for minors with adult supervision

UTMA accounts

  • Hold cash, stocks, bonds, and other assets.
  • Transfers to the child at the legal age. Age varies by state.

Roth IRA for minors

  • Child must have earned income.
  • Retirement withdrawals may be tax free under IRS rules. Early uses have conditions.
