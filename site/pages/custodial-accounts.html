Custodial Accounts

Parents and guardians can manage assets for a minor until they reach adulthood.

UTMA Roth IRA for minors Pros Cautions

UTMA accounts

  • Uniform Transfers to Minors Act.
  • Can hold cash, stocks, bonds, and other assets.
  • Transfers to the child at the legal age. Age depends on state.

Roth IRA for minors

  • Child must have earned income.
  • Earnings can be tax‑free for retirement withdrawals under IRS rules.
  • Some early withdrawal uses are allowed. Rules apply.

Pros

  • Broad investment choices.
  • Teaches saving and investing habits.

Cautions

  • Assets may affect financial aid.
  • Child gets full control at the legal age.
