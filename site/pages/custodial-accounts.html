Custodial Accounts

Parents/guardians manage assets for a minor until adulthood.

UTMA

  • Uniform Transfers to Minors Act.
  • Can hold cash, stocks, bonds, and more.
  • Transfers to child at legal age (varies by state).

Roth IRA for minors

  • Child needs earned income.
  • Tax-free retirement withdrawals under IRS rules.
  • Some early uses allowed; rules apply.
