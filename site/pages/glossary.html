Glossary

TermMeaning
OGAOne Grand Account. New federal account for kids born on or after January 1, 2025.
Tax deferredTaxes are paid later, usually when money is withdrawn.
UTMAUniform Transfers to Minors Act. A custodial account for minors.
529 PlanState sponsored account for education expenses.
IRAIndividual Retirement Account. A way to invest for retirement.
ContributionThe money you add to an account.
CompoundingGrowth that earns more growth over time.
WithdrawalMoney taken out of an account.
PenaltyA fee or tax owed for using funds in a way that breaks program rules.
RolloverMoving funds from one account to another within tax rules.
