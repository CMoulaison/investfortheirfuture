One Grand Account

Give kids a head start that compounds for decades

Eligibility is for children born on or after January 1, 2025. The government seeds $1,000. Families can add up to $5,000 per year. Growth is tax‑deferred.

What it is

A tax‑advantaged account designed to help families start early. The money is invested and grows over time.

Advantages

  • $1,000 initial seed from the government.
  • Tax‑deferred growth on earnings.
  • Simple structure for long‑term saving.

At age 18

  • Option to roll into a Traditional IRA.
  • Or use for approved purposes: college/trade school, first home, business startup, marriage, or large medical expenses.
