One Grand Account

Wealth starts at birth. OGAs open July 4, 2026. Growth is tax-deferred.

Eligibility How it works Uses after 18 Age 30/31 rules Calculator FAQ

Eligibility

  • Children born on or after Jan 1, 2025.
  • Accounts available starting July 4, 2026.

How it works

  • Federal seed: $1,000 at setup.
  • Private contributions: up to $5,000/year (after-tax).
  • Employer contributions: up to $2,500/year (counts toward $5,000 total) — pending final IRS/Treasury guidance.
  • Investments: low-cost index-tracking funds/ETFs.

Uses after 18

  • Education or job training.
  • First-time home purchase.
  • Major medical or disability expenses.

Note: “Starting a business” is cited in some summaries but not yet confirmed in regulations. Weddings and general life events are not qualified uses.

Age 30/31 rules

Before age 30, non-qualified withdrawals may face a 10% additional tax plus regular income tax on earnings. At age 31, the account may be treated as distributed if unused. Awaiting final Treasury/IRS guidance.

FAQ
