One Grand Accounts (OGAs)

Wealth starts at birth. OGAs give eligible children a head start toward long‑term security.

Eligibility How it works At age 18 Long‑term growth Calculator FAQ

Eligibility

  • Child must be born on or after January 1, 2025.
  • Account is opened in the child’s name with a parent or guardian.

How it works

  • Government seeds $1,000 at setup.
  • Family can contribute up to $5,000 per year.
  • Growth is tax‑deferred.

At age 18

  • Option to roll into a Traditional IRA.
  • Or use for approved purposes (college, training, first home, business, marriage, large medical expenses).

Long‑term growth

Money can keep growing past 18. We also model out to age 60 to show the power of time.
