NestFund Starter Guide

This 5–7 minute guide helps you pick a first step and schedule simple weekly deposits. When you join, you’ll also get our 13‑week email course (two emails per week).

1) What is an OGA?

An OGA is a federal account with a $1,000 seed for eligible births (2025–2028). Contributions begin July 4, 2026. Growth is tax-deferred.

2) Pick your weekly amount

Open the calculator on the OGA page and test $10, $25, $50 per week with and without the $1,000 seed and $5,000/yr cap.

3) Set up your routine

Automate weekly or twice‑weekly deposits from a checking account. Use calendar reminders.

4) At age 18

Use funds for education/job training, first home, or major medical expenses. Non‑qualified uses can face taxes/penalties.

5) Age 30/31 rules

Before 30, non‑qualified withdrawals may have a 10% additional tax plus income tax on earnings. At 31, any remaining balance may be treated as distributed (awaiting final guidance).
